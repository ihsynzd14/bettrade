How Bettrade works

Three data streams converge into a single execution pipeline.

01

Genius Sports delivers the signal

Live match events — goals, cards, dangerous attacks, momentum shifts — arrive via Ably real-time channels at under 250ms latency. Bettrade processes the raw payload instantly.

02

Betfair provides the market

Simultaneously, the Betfair Exchange Stream API feeds live odds, depth of book, and traded volume for matched markets. Bettrade maintains a real-time local order book cache.

03

The bot executes the trade

When a match signal meets a market condition, the strategy engine fires. Orders are placed via Betfair REST API, confirmed, and tracked — all within the same tick.
